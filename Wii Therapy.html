Wii Therapy: enhance your mobility

Video games have a lot of potential on a cognitive level, and can also serve as a rehabilitation tool. There are video consoles that have platforms such as the Wii Balance Board that allow the improvement of mobility in the joints, balance, visual-manual coordination, physical resistance and reflexes. Some games also allow group participation of people with different disabilities, encouraging inclusiveness. "Wii Therapy" should be practiced in socio-health centres or similar, and always under the supervision of physiotherapists to make the most out of it.
